RATAN NAVAL TATA

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist philanthropist and former chairman of tata sons.He was a chairman of tata group from 1990 to 2012,and an interim chairman from October 2016 through February 2017.he contributes to head its charitable trusts.Ratan tata is also a prolific investor and has made numerous investments in several startups.
